Event Production

Executive Director

Survivor UCF

I co-founded Survivor UCF in 2023 and built it from the ground up into one of the largest student organizations at the University of Central Florida. Under my leadership, the organization earned the UCF Impact Award for Best Large-Scale Event. As Executive Director, I designed the entire structure of the org and oversaw four committees: a casting committee that interviewed around 70 applicants per event, a challenge committee that built and sourced all of our original challenges, a social media and production committee that filmed and created content at every event, and an events committee that ran our fundraisers and social programming.

Fundraising

Charity Auctions

Every season we create custom buffs and props for the cast. We ended up with extra materials from each season, so we started running charity auctions to sell them off and raise money for the club. Members bid on season buffs, props, and other items from past seasons. It turned into a fun event on its own and a good way to keep the community engaged between seasons.

How It Worked

Season Merch Goes to Auction

We make custom buffs and props for every season. The extras went to members who wanted a piece of the season they played in or watched.

Event Assistant

Strike Magazine

I worked as an event assistant for Strike Magazine, an Orlando-based fashion and arts publication. My role was helping produce events that promoted the magazine and elevated its presence in the local creative scene. I handled logistics, coordinated with vendors, managed setup, and kept things running on the day of each event.

Public Relations

PR Intern

Toho Water Authority

I intern in the Public Relations department at Toho Water Authority, one of the largest water utilities in Florida. The job puts me across different departments, capturing what they do and turning it into content the public actually sees. I shoot photos and video, write copy, show up to outreach events, and help manage what goes out on their social channels.

📷

Content Creation

I go out with different departments, capture what they are working on, and turn it into social media posts and internal communications. Everything from field visits to team spotlights.

🌐

Community Outreach

I go out to events around the county and represent Toho in the community. That has meant helping with water conservation lessons at elementary schools, handing out water at 5K marathons, and showing up wherever Toho has a presence with the public.

👥

Cross-Department Work

Working in public relations means staying connected to every part of the organization. I reach out to other departments when I need information to write articles, capture photos, or produce video content. It requires building relationships across the organization and knowing how to translate technical work into something the public can understand.
